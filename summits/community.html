This unique event will gather a selective group of lead researchers from distinct workflow management system efforts, in which we seek to identify areas of need and open challenges from the workflow community. This event will be the first of a series of focused meetings, and will be key for defining the strategic goals for these upcoming meetings with the science community and workflow developers.
